# Healyx Digital

Healyx Digital is dedicated to improving the quality of healthcare delivery by enhancing the supporting systems that providers rely on. Our platform offers streamlined Electronic Health Record (EHR) solutions to automate patient management, scheduling, and record-keeping. We help healthcare providers focus on delivering high-quality care while we take care of the operational complexities.

## Purpose
At Healyx Digital, our mission is to empower healthcare providers by simplifying their administrative tasks, ensuring data integrity, and improving patient outcomes. Our solutions help healthcare teams manage appointments, patient records, and billing efficiently, reducing errors and saving time. We provide both standard and customized solutions to meet the diverse needs of small practices and large enterprises alike.

## Features
- **Automated Appointment Scheduling:** Easily manage patient appointments and reduce administrative workload.
- **Patient Chart Pop-Up:** A streamlined interface for viewing and updating patient charts quickly, reducing clicks and improving provider workflow.
- **Custom Solutions for Enterprises:** Tailored solutions designed for larger healthcare organizations to meet specific operational needs.
- **Transparent Pricing & Unlimited Support:** No hidden fees, with ongoing support to ensure smooth operation and scaling.

## Technologies Used
- **Backend:** Xano
- **Frontend:** Webflow
- **Custom Code:** GitHub (POSTs to Xano)
- **Data Management & Analytics:** Power BI, SQL, and other analytics tools
- **Healthcare Compliance:** HIPAA, data encryption, and industry standards for data protection

## Key Services
- **EHR Platform:** Automates patient intake, scheduling, record-keeping, and document uploads to enhance provider-patient interactions.
- **Billing & Treatment Management:** Simplifies billing processes, tracking treatment plans, and ensuring compliance with industry standards.
- **Customizable Solutions:** Designed for enterprises and larger organizations to provide unique workflows, custom pricing, and additional features.
- **Ongoing Support & Onboarding:** Comprehensive onboarding and continuous support to ensure users get the most out of the platform.

## Installation
To get started with Healyx Digital:

1. **Sign Up:** Register for an account on our platform.
2. **Set Up Your Practice:** Customize the platform according to your practice's needs.
3. **Integrate with Existing Systems:** Use our API to seamlessly integrate with your current EHR or other healthcare systems.
4. **Support:** Contact our dedicated team for onboarding assistance or any ongoing needs.
